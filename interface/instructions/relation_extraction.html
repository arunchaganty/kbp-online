First of all, welcome :)

The focus of this task is to find relations between entities in the text.

Steps

  1. First you will be given mentions of two entities and the sentence containing these will be highlighted.
  2. You have to figure out if these mentions are related and if so then how. Note that relation should be explicitly mentioned within the given sentence.
  3. For the two highlighted entities possible relations are shown as buttons and hovering you can hover on them to verify if it sounds correct
  4. After you make the correct choice, you'll be given the next pair of entities

What you see

  1. All extracted relations are shown in the right pane for your reference. If you made a mistake, they can be clicked upon and edited
  2. It is also possible to go to previous pairs of mentions to correct them by pressing on Back button

Common Pitfalls

  • If more than one relation seems to hold, pick the most appropriate one.
